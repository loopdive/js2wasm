name: Maintain project diary continuously
description: Append learnings, progress, incidents to plan/diary.md with date/time as they happen
type: feedback
---

Maintain a continuous diary at `plan/diary.md`. Append new entries with date and time whenever:
- A learning or insight is discovered
- Progress is made (merges, test results)
- An incident occurs (regressions, lost work, agent misbehavior)
- A process change is made (new checklists, role changes)

Format: `## YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM — Title` followed by bullet points.

**Why:** User wants a running log of what happened and what was learned, not just final results. Helps with retrospectives and onboarding new sessions.

**How to apply:** After any significant event, append to the diary before moving on. Keep entries concise — 2-5 bullet points each.
