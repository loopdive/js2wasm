## Key architectural finding

The "Wasm closures aren't JS-callable from host imports" gap blocks multiple host-fallback paths:
- `Array.from(items, mapFn)` mapFn dispatch (#1339)
- `Array.prototype.X.call(arrayLike, cb)` thisArg threading (#1358 plan item 2)
- Host-fallback for `Reflect.apply` / `.call()` patterns

This was filed as **#1382** (structural gap) by the team during my session. Future per-feature workarounds are fragile until #1382 lands a foundational bridge.
